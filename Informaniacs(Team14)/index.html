About

Nami has identified that 1 in 3 students may battle a mental illness. Some students are dealing with mental helath conditions and are either not even aware of it or are suffering in silence. Mental health can have an enourmous effect on your daily life such as your sleeping,eating, or study habits. Mental health is of utmost importance at such a vulnerable point in a students life.

Students cannot appropriately deal with their mental health conditions on their own. They can often intensify their emotions even more overwhelm themselves with the possibility of bettering themselves that they neglect the issue all together. PSYCAD is unable to help these same students because many of them are not aware of their services being offered as well as the fact that interaction and feedback between counsellors and students is necessary.

Our Services

Students Mobile: Mind over matter offers an appointment feature that allows students to make consultaions with their counsellors online,journal their emotions and thoughts in an e-diary, and track their moods daily. MOM also offers students a list of exercises that help them cope with thier mental health upon prescription. A student also has the ability to keep track of their own medication.
Counsellors Web: Mind over matter allows counsellors to track and monitor progress of students, view all their appointments with students, view a students profile whereby they can monitor e-diary flags, and precribe activites. The web also includes a functionlaity for counsellors to take notes during consultaiton sessions and provide a progress rating based on the students mental health progression and view the students overall activity on the app.
